### What You Will Learn

- Number operations and calculations
- Patterns, functions, and algebra

### Study Tips

1. **Review regularly** - Study a little each day, not just before tests
2. **Practice actively** - Do exercises, don't just read
3. **Ask questions** - Clarify anything you don't understand
4. **Use multiple resources** - Textbooks, workbooks, online resources

## Assessment Guidelines

### Foundation Phase Assessment (Grades R-3)

- **Grade R:** 100% formative assessment (no formal exams)
- **Grades 1-3:** School-Based Assessment (SBA) 70% + Final Assessment 30%
- **Tasks:** 16 assessment tasks per subject per year
- **Focus:** Continuous observation, informal testing, practical tasks
